Early Life and Education:

Born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, Elon Reeve Musk showed an early aptitude for technology and entrepreneurship. He developed a passion for computers and programming at a young age, teaching himself how to code and creating a video game at just 12 years old. Musk attended Pretoria Boys High School and later moved to Canada to attend Queen's University, eventually transferring to the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned two bachelor's degrees in physics and economics.
